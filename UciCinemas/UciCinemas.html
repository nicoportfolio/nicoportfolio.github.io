UCI Cinemas Website Redesign

Redesigning for improving usability

QUICK NAVIGATION
Tasks Main Problems Redesign

Following a thorough analysis, we proposed a website and App redesign for UCI Cinemas, a cineplex company currently operating in Italy, Germany, Brasil and Portugal. I contributed mostly in redesigning the desktop website, but also in the research part and in the benchmarking, analyzing the main features of the local competitor.

The goal of the new design is to improve the usability of the platform, thus effort has been spent in simplyfing the structure and the layout. Amongst the changes, we decided to include a ticket management page, which was missing in the old website, and a search bar for a quicker and versatile research of movie titles and venues.

Redesign

Search Bar

The search bar serves as a replacement to the "quick booking" menu and helps the user find what he needs without browsing the whole website. The search bar is dynamic and contextual to the input query, so that the seach results will adapt to the different needs of the personas we defined.

For instance the family father may want to search available movies in a specific date in the weekend, while the fandom may look for a specific genre or a specific movie. The suggested keywords help the user discover its functions. Moreover, if the user is logged in, it filters the results by the chosen favourite venue.

It can search for: Dates, Titles, Venues, Cities, Genres, Mood, Coming soon, Language (O.V.)
