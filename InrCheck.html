Research Definition UI

A personal project born from my curiosity in medication reminders app. My chosen brief was to design an app dedicated to patients following a specific medication routine, who may benefit from a more tailored experience than the one offered by generic pill reminders. My choice was to work on heart disease patients on anticoagulant medications. For these patients, the current offering of digital products in the market seems to be profoundly deficient despite the relative high amount undergoing this therapy.
